# Asterfold — dense new-tab directions

Generated 19 July 2026 for the 1920×1080 Chrome new-tab surface.

## Fixed product constraints

- Preserve Pages → Boards → Bookmarks.
- Keep roughly 100 bookmarks visible in the first viewport.
- Do not use page scrolling or nested scrolling.
- Keep Page navigation visible as tabs.
- Expose exactly one standalone control on the main screen: Settings.
- Leave creation, search, drag-and-drop, and item actions to keyboard shortcuts,
  contextual interactions, and secondary Settings surfaces.
- Favor restrained Apple-like typography, spacing, hierarchy, and materials.

## Directions

1. **Spatial Shelves** — five horizontal board shelves with compact title and
   hostname cells. Best balance of density and immediate hierarchy.
2. **Quiet Ledger** — five vertical board columns with twenty numbered rows
   each. Highest scan speed for title-heavy collections.
3. **Icon Atlas** — one 100-item icon atlas grouped by softly tinted board
   fields. Most approachable and visually memorable.
4. **Spatial Map** — unequal board territories sized as a calm workspace map.
   Best when boards differ substantially in importance or size.
5. **Editorial Directory** — warm, typographic multi-column reference index.
   Strongest reading-oriented and knowledge-library character.
6. **Signal Strips** — five icon-first horizontal board strips. Most compact
   and fastest for users who recognize bookmarks by favicon.
